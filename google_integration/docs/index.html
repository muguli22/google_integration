Google Connect

Stay connected to google

Sync Events and Contacts between ERPNext and Google

Install

From your site

To install this app, login to your site and click on "Installer". Search for Google Connect and click on "Install"

Using Bench

Go to your bench folder and setup the new app

$ bench get-app google_integration https://github.com/frappe/google_integration
$ bench new-site testsite
$ bench --site testsite install-app google_integration

Login to your site to configure the app.
